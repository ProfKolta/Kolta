Vision

Most people associate innovation with idea generation, however, true innovation is not only about generating ideas, but having a process for implementation. NY State Innovation Network is a platform that enables the entire lifecycle of innovation – from ideation to execution. Through a platform that integrates multiple public-private collaborative ecosystems, the Innovation Network strengthens local, regional and state economic viability, creates sustainable jobs, and support emerging entrepreneurial business. The innovation network will provide an infrastructure to address society’s major problems in such areas as healthcare, transportation, education, and urban development.
